From Halfpipe to “Superpipe” Over the years, the halfpipe has evolved into an industry standard “Superpipe.” Yes, its walls have gradually gotten taller, but that height has enabled a smoother geometry: Now, the entire half-pipe is essentially one huge transition with no flat spots or sudden impacts.
